Areas of concern — EEW COMP3B project
1. Supplier delivery delays (VAGB — plates)
Observation: Recovery noted in VAGB plate releases this week, following decreased productivity since early 2026.
Impact: Sustained downward trend persists for all PO items. VAGB forecasts imminent ramp-up, but previous shortfall caused ~2 week slippage vs VAGB Statement timeline.
Current status: 3,526 plates (78.32%) delivered vs Saipem PO quantity of 4,502 pcs (delta –21.68%).
Action plan: Focus on expediting plate deliveries and increase coordination with VAGB.
2. Release of line pipes (EEW)
Concern: Project progressing, though at a significantly slower rate compared to previous months.
Impact: Timely release of line pipes critical for offshore riser platform construction. Delays could halt welding, coating and pipeline assembly.
Current status: Release rate in accordance with production, average discrepancy of 2.6% for pipes inspected and released on PO.
PO discrepancy: IQS/Saipem dataset does not align with EEW records (referenced to Sumitomo PO).
MPFB delivery: Completed. Line pipe production: ongoing, very slowly.
